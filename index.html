Tesla's Cybertruck will be more expensive than expected. Elon Musk explains why. "A lot has changed", says Musk.

Tesla's Cybertruck is scheduled to go into mass production in mid-2023 but it will be more expensive than the starting price revealed in 2019, according to Tesla CEO Elon Musk.
